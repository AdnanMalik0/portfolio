# Hotel Revenue and Customer Analysis

Source GitHub Repository: <https://github.com/AdnanMalik0/Hotel-revenue-and-customer-analysis>

## Snapshot

This project is an interactive Power BI analysis of hotel revenue and customer behaviour using booking and operational data from January 2018 to September 2020. It is organised around business questions rather than generic charting.

## Goal

The goal was to understand hotel performance, strengths, weaknesses, and operational patterns, then turn those findings into a dashboard that supports planning and business improvement.

## What Was Built

- A Power BI dashboard for hotel revenue and customer analysis
- Analysis of overall, yearly, and monthly revenue
- Reservation and cancellation analysis
- Guest-volume and market-segment analysis
- Discount, meal-revenue, and nationality analysis for commercial interpretation

## Business Questions Explored

- overall revenue
- year-wise and month-wise revenue
- total reservations and cancellations
- guest volumes and average daily guests
- revenue by market segment
- discount behaviour by segment
- meal-revenue contribution
- customer nationality trends

## Evidence Included

- A completed Power BI dashboard file for interactive business analysis
- A reusable dashboard template
- The supporting hotel revenue dataset used for the analysis
- Dashboard visuals showing the final reporting output

## Result / Outcome

The analysis helped identify patterns and areas for improvement, supported future planning, and made it easier to compare more profitable and less profitable market segments. It also framed hotel performance in a way that is directly useful for operational review.

## Portfolio Takeaway

This is a strong BI project because it is built around decision-making questions a hotel business would actually care about. It shows dashboard design aligned to revenue, customer behaviour, and commercial planning.
